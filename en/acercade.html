About us






I am Montse Nzang Camps, founder of JAB Consultants, and for over 20 years, I’ve been helping companies achieve their business goals.


My passion for sales and business challenges has led me to collaborate with both emerging startups and multinational companies, providing strategic solutions to grow and establish themselves in the competitive B2B market. I’ve led sales teams and designed sales strategies that have propelled businesses in diverse sectors such as technology, fitness, and services. As an entrepreneur, I’ve experienced firsthand the challenges of building a project from scratch, which allows me to deeply understand the needs of businesses.


The degree in Biology has given me strong analytical skills, and my Master’s in Commercial Management and Marketing complements my extensive experience in sales.


I firmly believe that business success lies in a well-defined, realistic commercial strategy backed by flawless execution. I would love to learn more about your business and explore how we can collaborate to take it to the next level!
