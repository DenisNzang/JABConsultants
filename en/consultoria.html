B2B Commercial consulting






In the B2B sector, companies face a competitive environment where specialization and strategy are essential for success. At JAB Consultants, we turn these challenges into opportunities. Our B2B Sales Consultancy is designed to help your business not only adapt to the market but thrive in it and stand out.


Challenges in B2B


The B2B sector poses unique challenges that can hinder your business growth if not properly managed:


  • Limited market niches: A small pool of potential clients means every opportunity counts.

  • Difficulty reaching decision-makers: Accessing key decision-makers is complicated and requires a strategic approach.

  • Demanding buyers: B2B clients are highly analytical and demand solutions that truly address their needs.

  • Long sales cycles: Purchases involve careful consideration, extending the sales process.

  • Building trust:: Establishing trust is critical to closing deals and fostering customer loyalty.

  • Hybrid and omnichannel sales models: Balancing in-person and digital interactions requires constant adaptation of sales strategies.


These challenges can lead to issues such as ineffective strategies, slow sales growth, low conversion and retention rates, decreased team performance, demotivation, and high turnover. At JAB Consultants, we help you turn these situations around, offering an objective and assertive external strategic perspective

.

Our methodology


Our consulting service follows four key phases:


  1. Analysis:
    We examine your company’s situation, industry, competitors, sales structure (both technical and human), needs, and goals. This phase helps us weigh the factors that impact your sales performance.

  2. Diagnosis:
    Based on the analysis, we create a diagnosis that identifies problems and areas for improvement. We pinpoint the factors affecting sales performance and highlight opportunities for growth.

  3. Proposal:
    Together, we develop an action plan tailored to your needs. This includes a sales roadmap aligned with your business environment, featuring actionable strategies designed for sustainable results.

  4. Implementation:
    Our commitment doesn’t end with the proposal. We support you throughout the plan’s implementation, ensuring that each step delivers tangible results. We monitor progress using key performance indicators to measure objectives achieved and identify potential deviations.
