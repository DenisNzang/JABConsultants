Training






In the competitive world of B2B sales, having a well-prepared team makes the difference between meeting your goals or falling behind... or failing altogether. Training not only enhances the skills and competencies of the sales team, but also directly impacts productivity, customer satisfaction, and ultimately, the company’s financial results.


Benefits of training your sales team


Some of the advantages of training sales teams are:


  • Improved sales skills

  • Enhanced negotiation abilities

  • Increased productivity

  • Adaptation to market changes

  • Higher customer retention

  • Better communication

  • Boosted innovation, confidence, and motivation

  • Alignment with business strategy

At JAB Consultants we understand the challenges SMEs and startups face when selling B2B products and services. That’s why we have designed a series of training programs tailored to the specific needs of these sales teams, providing them with the tools necessary to stand out in the market.


Our B2B sales training programs


  1. Effective prospecting techniques: Find and attract the right clients
    Without proper prospecting, it’s challenging to maintain a steady flow of opportunities.

    Our training on effective prospecting techniques is designed to help your team identify and reach potential clients efficiently. We explore market research methods, the use of digital tools like AI, and the creation of personalized approaches that increase conversion chances.

    The goal is for your team to fill their sales funnel with qualified leads.

  2. Consultative sales strategies: Putting the customer at the center of the conversation
    B2B sales go beyond offering a product or service. It’s about deeply understanding the client’s needs and providing tailored solutions

    In our consultative sales strategies training, we teach participants how to ask the right questions to identify their clients’ true needs. They learn various negotiation strategies and how to adapt to each client’s requirements. From negotiating styles to handling difficult conversations, the focus is always on achieving mutually beneficial agreements.

    Additionally, they learn how to become trusted advisors, which is crucial in a hybrid sales environment.

  3. Sales closing: The key to closing deals
    Getting a potential client to say "yes" requires specific skills.

    In our sales closing training, we teach your team proven strategies to close deals effectively. We focus on persuasion tactics, handling objections, and asking questions that guide the client toward a buying decision.

    By the end of the training, participants are equipped to convert more opportunities into sales.

  4. Personal branding: Stand out in the market
    In today’s digital world, personal branding is essential for any salesperson.

    This training helps them develop their personal brand by teaching how to present themselves attractively on social networks like LinkedIn and build a professional profile that stands out.

    Participants learn how to position themselves as experts in their field, building greater trust and credibility among potential clients.

If you want your B2B sales team to be more efficient, competitive, and prepared for market challenges, our training programs are the solution you need. We also offer customized training for specific needs.


By offering training that addresses key skills and relevant topics, businesses ensure their teams are well-prepared to face market challenges and achieve their sales goals.
