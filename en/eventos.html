Events






At JAB Consultants we believe corporate events are far more than just meetings. They are a powerful tool in the marketing and sales strategies of any SME or startup. We design them to enhance your company’s reputation while creating a positive and lasting impact on your team.


Locations and activities


We invite you to discover Priorat, a region of unique and enchanting landscapes. Located less than an hour from the airports and high-speed train stations of Tarragona and Lleida, this land of vineyards, olive trees, and almond orchards provides the perfect setting for your events. Far from the urban hustle, the natural environment here inspires and connects.


Priorat is not just a rural destination; it’s a place steeped in history, culture, and modernity. Picturesque villages with cutting-edge infrastructure and a wide range of activities make this region the ideal location for any corporate event. Hosting your event here offers a unique opportunity for your team and clients to experience something truly special.


At JAB Consultants we organize corporate events in rural settings to provide an experience that blends work and relaxation in an inspiring atmosphere. This change of scenery fosters creativity and strengthens team cohesion, resulting in an unforgettable and impactful event. Activities can include wine or olive oil tasting, rock climbing, yoga, stargazing, hiking in Montsant Natural Park, and more. These experiences connect and transform, offering various levels of intensity and duration tailored to the participants’ profiles.


We handle the management and coordination of all event stages, including venue selection, technical requirements, transportation, catering, accommodation, communication, and marketing.


Types of events


We design corporate events tailored to the specific needs of each company:


  • Kick-off meetings and product launches: A perfect way to start new projects or unveil products in an inspiring setting.

  • Seminars and symposiums: Spaces where attendees can exchange knowledge and build professional relationships.

  • Work meetings: For teams needing a distraction-free, motivating environment to work effectively.

  • Team building and incentive activities: Experiences that foster cohesion and improve team communication.

  • Family days: Moments for employees and their families to bond and strengthen connections.

At JAB Consultants we combine our deep knowledge of Priorat with our expertise in event planning to ensure every detail is covered. Our commitment is to deliver an event that inspires, motivates, and adds value to both your team and your company.


Tell us your vision and we’ll help you transform your next corporate event into an unforgettable experience.
